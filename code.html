Yeat

Noah Olivier Smith (born February 26, 2000), known professionally as Yeat is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer.He gained recognition in mid-2021 after the release of his mixtape 4L and debut studio album Up 2 Më, with tracks from the latter, including "Monëy So Big", "Gët Busy", "Turban", and "Twizzy Rich" gaining popularity. In 2022, he released his second studio album, 2 Alivë, and the EP Lyfë. Yeat is recognized as being part of a new wave of SoundCloud rap which is characterized by synth-based instrumentals reminiscent of Georgia rapper Playboi Carti, called "Rage".
